About Our Institution

SSAMC is dedicated to preserving African traditional medicine while providing modern vocational training to empower individuals and communities.

Our Mission

To provide accessible education in African traditional medicine and vocational skills that empower individuals to become self-sufficient and contribute to community development.

Our Vision

A future where traditional African healing practices are preserved and integrated with modern vocational skills to create sustainable livelihoods across communities.

Our Values

Respect for tradition, commitment to excellence, community empowerment, and sustainable development through education.

Our Programs

All our programs are 6-month certification courses designed to provide practical skills for immediate employment or entrepreneurship.

African Medicine

6-month certification program in traditional healing practices and herbal medicine.

Basic Plumbing

6-month vocational training in residential and commercial plumbing systems.

Welding

6-month metalwork certification focusing on various welding techniques.

Soap Making

6-month artisanal production course for natural and commercial soap products.

Candle Making

6-month creative design course for decorative and aromatic candles.

Sewing

6-month garment construction program for fashion and textile skills.
