About Us

Learn about our mission, vision, and the values that drive our institution

Our Mission

To preserve and promote African traditional medicine while providing vocational training that empowers individuals with practical skills for economic self-sufficiency and community development.

Our Vision

To become a leading institution in Africa that bridges traditional healing practices with contemporary vocational skills, fostering holistic community development and cultural preservation.

Our History

Founded in 2010, SSA Medical Center has evolved from a traditional healing practice to a comprehensive institution offering both traditional medicine and vocational training programs.

Our Programs

Explore our diverse range of vocational training programs

Organic Farming

Learn sustainable agricultural practices and organic farming techniques for food security and income generation.

Herbal Medicine Production

Master the art of preparing traditional medicines, tinctures, and herbal products for health and wellness.

Food Processing & Preservation

Acquire skills in food processing, packaging, and preservation to reduce post-harvest losses and create value-added products.

Soap & Cosmetics Production

Learn to produce natural soaps, cosmetics, and personal care products using local ingredients and traditional recipes.
