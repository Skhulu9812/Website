About SSAMC ✎

Discover our mission, vision, and commitment to empowering African communities through education ✎

Our Mission ✎

Sakhokuhle School of African Medicine and Crafting provides highly flexible programs geared towards equipping and empowering African Health Practitioners who will change the world. ✎

All courses are rooted in ancestral knowledge and are available in various formats to everyone who meets our entrance requirements. ✎

Our Vision ✎

To be recognized as an international center for excellence that is both rooted in its environment and sensitive to the challenges of human progress. ✎

To foster a culture of teaching, learning, and research excellence as a basic minimum requirement for being a site of knowledge generation. ✎

Our Programs ✎

Comprehensive training programs designed to empower you with practical skills and knowledge ✎

Traditional Medicine ✎

Comprehensive training in traditional African medicine, herbal remedies, and holistic healing practices. ✎

Herbalism ✎

Tailoring & Design ✎

Develop professional cooking skills and learn about traditional African cuisine. ✎
